Student Projects - Lukas Schmid


I'm passionate about the education of the next generation of scientists and engineers. Next to teaching exercise classes for lectures, I mainly contribute to this through the supervision of Master, Semester, and Bachelor Theses.

I regularly upload project proposals to Sirop, which are also featured on the student projects page of the Autonomous Systems Lab at ETH Zurich. I also accept proactive applications of highly motivated students. If you are interested in working on a project in the fields of active perception and long-term scene understanding please send a short CV and transcript to schmluk@ethz.ch.
